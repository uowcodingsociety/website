About Us

The CodeSoc exec team aims to enhance your Warwick experience by focusing on advancing your programming skills. Whether you're just starting out or come with years of experience, we'll meet you in the middle, offering opportunities to enhance and build upon your knowledge. CodeSoc was founded to give all Warwick University students an opportunity to develop their programming abilities further, regardless of the course they study. We're still growing, but we're committed to our goal. We look forward to having you with us.
